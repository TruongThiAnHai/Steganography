Hanoi is the capital of Vietnam. It covers an area of
3,328 square kilometres (1,285 sq mi). With an estimated
population of 8.12 million as of 2019, it is the second
largest city in Vietnam. The metropolitan area, encompassing
nine additional neighbouring provinces, has an estimated
population of 20 millions. Located in the nortwest of the
Red River Delta, Hanoi is the commercial, cultural, and
educational centre of Northern Vietnam.
